United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

The United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC) is a United Nations body whose mission is to promote and protect human rights around the world. This committee discusses and addresses various human rights issues globally.

Key Information:

  • Established: 2006
  • Headquarters: Geneva, Switzerland
  • Members: 47 United Nations member states

Current Topics:

  • Human rights implications of the COVID-19 pandemic
  • Rights of indigenous peoples
  • Climate change and human rights
